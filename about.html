About BTech Wala

BTech Wala Tiffin Center is dedicated to providing affordable, nutritious, and delicious meals to students pursuing engineering degrees.

We understand the struggles of hostel life and the need for home-cooked food. Our mission is to bring that comfort to every student's plate with our specially curated menu of traditional South Indian and North Indian dishes.

With a team of experienced cooks and a commitment to quality, we ensure every meal is prepared fresh and served with love. Since 2022, we've been serving thousands of satisfied students with consistent quality and affordable pricing.
